📖 About Proxi

Proxi is an iOS VPN client that routes all device traffic through a user-configured SOCKS5 or HTTPS proxy. It creates a system-wide VPN tunnel using Apple's NEPacketTunnelProvider API, allowing any app on your device to communicate through your chosen proxy server.

Proxi does not collect, log, or transmit any user traffic data. The VPN tunnel runs entirely on your device. Proxy servers are either provided by you or purchased through third-party providers.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Proxi?
Proxi is a VPN client for iOS that creates a system-wide VPN tunnel and routes all traffic through a SOCKS5 or HTTPS proxy you configure.
How do I use Proxi?
Enter your proxy server details (host, port, protocol) on the Proxy tab, then tap Connect. Proxi will create a VPN tunnel and route your device's traffic through the proxy. You can test the connection, monitor speed, and view tunnel logs from the app.
I'm having connection issues.
Verify your proxy server is running and reachable. Check the host, port, and authentication credentials. Use the built-in speed test to confirm connectivity. If issues persist, contact us at the email below with details from the Logs tab.
How do I purchase proxies?
Open the Purchases tab in the app. Select your desired proxy parameters (provider, country, quantity, period) and complete payment via cryptocurrency through NowPayments. Proxy credentials will be emailed to you after payment is confirmed.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Proxi runs entirely on your device. No traffic logs, usage data, or personal information are collected or transmitted by the app. Proxy credentials are stored locally and only if you choose to save them.
Does Proxi support subscriptions?
No. Proxi offers one-time proxy purchases with period options of 7, 14, 30, 60, or 90 days. There are no recurring subscriptions.
