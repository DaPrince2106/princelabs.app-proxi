❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Glass Calculator?
A beautifully designed all-in-one calculator with a premium glass-effect interface. It supports basic calculations, advanced scientific functions, unit conversions (13 categories), live currency exchange rates, and calculation history.
How do I switch between modes?
Tap the mode button (≡) on the display to open the mode picker. Select from Basic, Scientific, Converter, Graphing, or History. The interface adapts to each mode.
How does the unit converter work?
Switch to Converter mode, select a category (Length, Weight, Temperature, Volume, Area, Speed, Time, Digital Storage, Energy, Pressure, Fuel Economy, Angle, or Currency), choose your from/to units, and enter a value. Results appear instantly.
Are currency rates live?
Yes. The app fetches live exchange rates from open.er-api.com when you use the Currency converter. If the fetch fails, it falls back to 1:1 rates so the converter still functions.
Does the app collect my data?
No. Glass Calculator collects no personal data, no usage analytics, and no tracking information. All calculations are performed locally on your device.
How do I view calculation history?
Switch to History mode from the mode picker. Your last 100 calculations are stored with timestamps. Tap an entry to reload it, or clear all history with the trash button.
Is there a dark mode?
Yes. Tap the sun/moon icon in the top-right corner of the display to toggle between light and dark themes.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Glass Calculator respects your privacy. This policy describes how your information is handled when you use the app.

Data Collection
Glass Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device. No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs are included in the app.
Internet Access
The app only accesses the internet to fetch live currency exchange rates from open.er-api.com when you use the Currency converter. No device identifiers, usage data, or personal information are sent with these requests.
Local Storage
Calculation history (up to 100 entries) is stored locally in the app's memory and is not persisted to disk or shared with any third party. Clearing the history removes all stored data.
Third-Party Services
The app uses open.er-api.com to fetch live exchange rates. This service does not receive any personal information from the app. No other third-party services are used.
Children's Privacy
Glass Calculator is safe for all ages. It does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
